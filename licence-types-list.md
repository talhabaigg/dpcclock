# Complete list of licence / ticket / training types

Flattened list of every selectable type and sub-type on the "Add licence, ticket or training" form.

## 1. General construction induction card
- General construction induction card (White/Blue card)

## 2. Qualifications
- Trade certificate - Cert III in plastering
- Trade certificate - Cert III in carpentry
- USI transcript
- Other (free-text: Qualification name)

## 3. Tickets & licences
- **High risk work licence (HRWL)** — category:
  - Boom WP - above 11m
  - Forklift
  - Other (free-text: Other category name)
- **EWP - below 11m** — category:
  - Boom
  - Scissor
  - Vertical lift
  - VOC documentation
  - Other (free-text: Other category name)
- Telehandler
- Work safely at heights
- Manual handling
- Other (free-text: Ticket or licence name)

## 4. Short courses
- Workplace impairment training
- Asbestos awareness training
- Silica awareness training
- Gender equity training
- First Aid / CPR
- Other (free-text: Course name)

## 5. Health & safety representative training (HSR)
- Full course (5 days)
- Refresher

## 6. Quantitative fit test card
- Quantitative fit test card

---

## Totals

- 6 top-level categories
- 25 predefined sub-types (leaf nodes, excluding "Other" text-entry branches)
- 5 "Other" free-text branches (Qualifications, HRWL Category, EWP Category, Tickets & licences, Short courses)
- 2 category listboxes (HRWL and EWP under Tickets & licences) that allow multiple selections
